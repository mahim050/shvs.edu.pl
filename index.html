About SHVS

SHVS is a public state higher vocational school offering first-cycle (bachelor and engineer) programmes with a strong practical profile.

Our classes are conducted by experienced academics and professionals. Students benefit from modern laboratories, multimedia classrooms, and close cooperation with local companies and institutions.

Key Facts

  • Public higher education institution
  • Modern campus & specialist laboratories
  • Strong focus on employability
  • International projects and partnerships
